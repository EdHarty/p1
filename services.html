Child and Educational Psychologist

Anxiety

At times, anxiety can impact how your child thinks and what they do (actions and behaviours). When anxiety interferes with their home, school and social life, this is when professional help from a Child and Educational Psychologist may be needed.

Depression

Does your child experience persistent feelings of sadness. If so, psychological treatment may help ease depressive symptoms.

Sleep

At times, your child may struggle with sleep and nightmares. Professional therapy may be needed. Child & Educational Psychologists can develop strategies that make the night time routine feel more relaxed for children.
