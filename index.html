About

Hi, I'm Lisa, a committed and motivated Child and Educational Psychologist and a member of the Psychological Society of Ireland. I specialise in educational psychology assessments for children and adolescents. For years, I have supported children in preschools, primary schools and secondary schools. My diverse experiences to date have allowed me to gain direct experience in educational and healthcare settings. Get in touch today and let the journey begin.

Assessments

Educational Psychology Assessments: With this assessment, I will evaluate your child's cognitive and academic skills. Such assessments, will help me synthesise, use and share information to negotiate and develop action plans to address learning outcomes for children and young adults with diverse abilities and needs.

Therapy

Therapeutic Intervention: To help support Anxiety, Depression, Insomnia, Obsessive Compulsive Disorder and Grief. During such sessions, I will support the child and family/care giver to gain a better understanding of their current difficulties; look at causes and maintaining factors; and implications for treatment and goal setting.
